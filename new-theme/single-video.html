Cultural Flow - Wadi Wadi

by John | January 30, 2013 | Australia

A colourful comic short about Ghoulbert, a happy-go-lucky demon living a comfy suburban existance, thwarted by his innoncence in his attempts to do evil.

Ghoulbert wakes up on just another day in suburbia to be doing Satan’s evil bidding. Unfortunately for Ghoulbert, he’s a bit too innocent to be much of a demon, and try as he might, he just ends up doing good. This colourful comic short challenges fixed notions of morality as two missionaries pay him a visit, disrupting his comfy satanic suburban existence.

Shot on 16mm film on location in Melbourne, in early 2010, written and directed by John Doe, and featuring Paul Bourke, Kevin Newman and Zac Gower. Cinematography by Ellenor Argyropoulous and Production Design by Kathy Helme. Ghoulbert Gets Good on IMDB.
