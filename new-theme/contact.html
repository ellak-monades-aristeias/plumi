Plumi 4.5 Video Sharing
This is the demo site for Plumi, a free open source video sharing app for the web. Plumi is a Python package built on the Plone CMS. Features auto transcoding, HTML5 video player, video podcasting, upload via FTP, Creative Commons, Amara subtitling engine, video analytics and more.
